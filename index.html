ABOUT PIXEL DREAMS

Pixel Dreams is a dreamy pixel-art studio in your browser. Upload a video or use your webcam, then shape the look with palettes, dithering, edges, and temporal blending.

  • Temporal stability keeps static areas from flickering frame-to-frame
  • Dreamy curated palettes for morning, twilight, moonlight, and more
  • Preset looks for one-click style direction
  • Real-time controls for pixel scale, dithering, and edge accents

Everything runs client-side with JavaScript, WebGL shaders, and canvas (github code). Export stills or full videos whenever you find a look you love.

The project is free and open source (MIT). If it helps your creative workflow, a coffee keeps the experiments going.

If video export fails on your browser, use a fallback screen recorder like OBS Studio.

Your media never leaves your device. Processing is fully local and no uploads are sent to any server.
